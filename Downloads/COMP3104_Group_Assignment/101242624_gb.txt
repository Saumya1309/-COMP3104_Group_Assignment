George Brown College is located in Toronto Downtown, ON, Canada. 

George Brown College was established in 1967. 

The college offers a wide variety of part-time and full-time programs to all its students.

The college offers certificates, diplomas, undergraduate and graduate degrees and the main popular academic fields are Early childhood leadership, 
Art and design, Business management, Community services, Construction and Engineering Technologies, Health Sciences, Hospitality, and 
Culinary Arts, Nursing, Finance, and Preparatory studies. 

This college is considered to be as one of the best colleges across the Canada.
